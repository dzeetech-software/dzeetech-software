Our Bottles: Locally Produced ,Fully Recyclable

At JENTAFLEX, we take full control of the packaging process by producing our own bottles in-house. Our bottle manufacturing unit is equipped to deliver high-quality, food-grade PET bottles that are lightweight, durable, and 100% recyclable. Beyond our Chidanz Water brand, we also supply custom bottles to other businesses in need of reliable packaging for their own products — including bottled water companies, beverage producers, and commercial resellers. Our production process ensures consistency, hygiene, and structural integrity at every stage. By manufacturing locally, we reduce dependency on imports, support local industries, and promote sustainability. We are also working toward long-term goals in plastic waste reduction and recycling initiatives. Whether you're a growing brand or an established supplier, JENTAFLEX provides bottle solutions designed for performance, safety, and environmental responsibility.

Sustainability & Recycling: Closing the Loop

At JENTAFLEX, sustainability is more than a goal — it’s a responsibility. As a manufacturer of plastic bottles and packaging materials, we recognize the impact our industry can have on the environment. That’s why we’re actively working to reduce waste, encourage recycling, and create a more circular production system. Our bottles are made from 100% recyclable PET materials, and we’re exploring partnerships to expand collection and recycling efforts within our local communities. We aim to support a closed-loop system where used bottles are repurposed, reused, or recycled efficiently — reducing environmental footprint and promoting cleaner surroundings. As we grow, we remain committed to innovation that balances functionality with eco-conscious practices. From raw material sourcing to post-consumer recycling initiatives, we’re building a future where quality production and environmental responsibility go hand in hand. At JENTAFLEX, sustainability isn’t a side project — it’s built into everything we do.
