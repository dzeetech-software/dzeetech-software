How We Produce It: Hygiene, Filtration & Freezing

At Chidanz Ice, quality starts with process. Our edible ice is produced using a carefully controlled system designed to ensure safety, purity, and consistency at every stage. We begin with a thorough filtration and purification process, removing impurities, odours, and contaminants from the water. This ensures that the base water meets food-grade standards before it even reaches the freezing stage. Once purified, the water enters our automated freezing system, where it is frozen under hygienic conditions to form clear, solid, consumable ice. The entire process is carried out in a clean environment with strict hygiene protocols, including routine equipment sterilization and packaging in sealed, food-safe bags. This method guarantees that every cube of Chidanz Ice is safe to consume — whether it’s going into a drink, preserving food, or being used for events. We don’t just make ice. We make clean, reliable ice you can trust every time.
